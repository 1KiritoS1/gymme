FAQ.

How can I book a workout class?

Booking a workout classe is simple for our members. Simply log into your Gymme account on our website and click "book class" button . You can book 3 days in advance at 9pm.

You can cancel your class online by logging back in and click "cancel booking".

Can I pay by cash for my membership?

You can change to a monthly Direct Debit the next time you are due to renew, so long as you have not previously defaulted on a Spotlight contract.

When you are in your renewal period you will be able to select a new method of payment.

What age do I need to be to join?

It is advised that 17-18 years is the best age where the benefits of working out at a gym can be achieved without any trouble.

It can lead to strong, muscular, lean and healthy physique in men and slim and healthy outline among women. Always be careful while joining a gym.

Are there any lockers?

Gym lockers provide secure storage for members and guests that need somewhere to store their sports bag, clothing and gym gear while they're working out.

How do I cancel my membership?

You may terminate your Membership at any time by cancelling the direct debit mandate with your bank. Your Membership will end with immediate effect, and no refund will be due.

Email membership@gmail.com and ask them to cancel your account.

Is there water available at the gym?

Staying hydrated is essential during workouts, so make sure to keep a bottle of water close by during your workout.

We recommend using a reusable water bottle as they are better for the environment and keep your water colder for longer.
